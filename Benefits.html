All You Need to Know about Sleep

Get sick less often

Immune system is your body's first-line defense against invaders like germs. It helps protect you from getting sick and promotes healing when you are unwell or injured. You can strengthen the immune system by getting enough sleep.

Stay at Healthy Weight

Sleep is essential to regulate Hormones that affect hunger and appetite. Appetite is not just a matter of stomach grumbling. The neurotransmitters, the chemical messengers control the appetite by allowing neurons (nerve cells) to communicate with one another. Ghrelin promotes hunger and leptin contributes to feeling of fullness. Getting enough sleep prevents dysregulation of these neurotransmitters which will cause excess calories.

Lower the Risk for Serious Health Problems (Heart Disease,Diabetes)

As getting enough sleep strengthen the immune system that is important defense against invaders like germs. The invaders include Bacteria, Viruses, Fungi that can cause infections and Parasites. Immune system is keeping those invaders out of the body and destroying them and healing damage to our body.

Reduce stress and improve Mood

Stress is a response to adverse and challenging circumstances and a response to daily life. It affects us emotionally, physically, and behaviorally. The right amount of stress can be a positive force that helps us to do our best and to keep alert and energetic. But too much can make us tense and anxious. A good night's sleep makes you able to tackle the day's stress more easily. When you are tired, you are less patient and more easily agitated, which can increase stress. As stress getting lower, your mood improves.

Think more Clearly and Do better at School and Work

Getting enough sleep help you feeling rested and energetic. When you are tired, you can not function at your best. Sleep helps you think more clearly, have quicker reflexes and focus better. Better sleep helps the brain make connections and solve problems, fueling the creative process. Without sleep, the brain struggles to form ideas and perform at its best.

Get along better with People

Loneliness is associated with poor sleep. A person become socially avoidant, keeping greater social distance from others. Also lack of sleep causes aggression. It causes you to be angrier and impatient. People who sleep poorly are more frequently aggressive toward their partners and friends. Our ability to turn down emotional upset is compromised when we don't get enough sleep. That means our anger can get out of control, making us more likely to blame and target others. Getting enough sleep allows us to have more self-control, help us empathize with others. So, if we know they are feeling sad, we can care about how she is doing and cheer her up.
